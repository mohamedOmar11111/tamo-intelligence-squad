# TAMO Protocol: /archive (Memory Preservation)

**Objective:** Capture and archive mission intelligence into the Shared Brain (Mem0) to facilitate cross-client pattern recognition.

## 1. Squad Configuration
*   **The Custodian (Lead):** Executes the `archive` skill and manages Mem0 synchronization.
*   **The Librarian:** Indexes reports into the local Context Vault.
*   **The Oracle:** Queries the Shared Brain for past-win patterns during mission briefings.

## 2. Operational Logic
1.  **Phase Alpha (Extraction):** Strips technical findings and ROI metrics from the mission output.
2.  **Phase Beta (Sync):** Updates the Global Fact Ledger with verified intelligence nodes.
3.  **Phase Gamma (Cross-Link):** Identifies similar patterns in other active client accounts for optimization.
