# TAMO Protocol: /demand (Forensic Research)

**Objective:** Extract sub-surface user signals and unfulfilled market requests from dark social and technical communities.

## 1. Squad Configuration
*   **The Demand Hunter (Lead):** Executes the `requesthunt` protocol across Reddit, X, and GitHub.
*   **The Signal Analyst:** Filters noise from high-intent signals.
*   **The Gap Architect:** Maps unfulfilled requests to technical implementation roadmaps.

## 2. Operational Logic
1.  **Phase Alpha (Crawl):** Scans niche-specific subreddits and social threads for "I wish," "How do I," and "Competitor X is missing..." patterns.
2.  **Phase Beta (Clustering):** Groups signals into psychological pain-point quadrants.
3.  **Phase Gamma (Extraction):** Identifies the highest-volume "Value Gaps."
