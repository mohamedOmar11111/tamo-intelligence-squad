# WIKI: Psychographic Trigger Mapping (PTM)
**Status:** ARCHITECT_PROTOCOL
**Authority:** TAMO Intelligence Squad

## 1. Beyond Demographic Targeting
Demographics (Age, Location, Gender) are surface-level variables. **Psychographic Trigger Mapping (PTM)** target's the user's "Internal Decision Architecture." We focus on the **Emotional Trigger Hierarchy (ETH)** to understand *why* a user converts, not just *who* they are.

## 2. The Pain-Point Shadow
Every successful market leader leaves a "Shadow"—a psychological area they are unable to serve due to their monolithic brand structure.
*   **The Shadow:** Identified via forensic scraping of user feedback and competitor copy.
*   **The Strike:** Engineering a narrative that targets the shadow with high-status alternatives.

## 3. Algorithmic Implementation
PTM is integrated directly into the **TAMO Profiler** agent logic. The agent scans competitor content to detect "Value-Gaps" and identifies where the messaging is misaligned with core user intent.
